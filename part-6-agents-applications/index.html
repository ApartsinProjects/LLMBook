Building Conversational AI using LLM and Agents

Part VI: Agents and Applications

Building autonomous AI agents, multi-agent systems, multimodal applications, and real-world LLM solutions with rigorous evaluation.

Part Overview

Part VI brings everything together into complete, autonomous systems. You will build AI agents that use tools, plan multi-step tasks, and reason about their actions. You will orchestrate multi-agent systems, work with multimodal models (vision, audio, video), build domain-specific LLM applications across industries, and design rigorous evaluation and observability infrastructure. This is where theory becomes product.

Chapters: 5 (Modules 21 through 25). The capstone of the technical content, drawing on every preceding part. Prepares you for the production and strategy discussions in Part VII.
