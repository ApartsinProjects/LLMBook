The Writing Team

"Managing a team of AI agents is like herding very intelligent cats."

Census The Multi-Agent Coordinator

This book was written, reviewed, and refined by a team of 42 specialized AI agents. Each agent brings a distinct area of expertise, personality, and editorial perspective to the production process. Some focus on technical accuracy; others on narrative flow, cognitive load, or visual design. Together they form a collaborative pipeline that plans, drafts, critiques, and polishes every chapter. The profiles below show the full roster. To learn more about each character's personality and the epigraphs they contribute throughout the book, visit the Wisdom Council.

Big Picture

The 42 agents are organized into functional groups: foundations specialists, architecture and optimization experts, application builders, training and adaptation engineers, retrieval and conversation designers, production and safety advisors, and cross-cutting editorial roles. Each agent reviewed every chapter that falls within its expertise.
