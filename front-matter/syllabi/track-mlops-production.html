FM.3: Course Syllabi

MLOps / Production Track

Prerequisites

DevOps experience (CI/CD, Docker, Kubernetes). Python packaging and testing. Chapters 10 through 11 for API basics. Cloud provider experience (AWS/GCP/Azure).

MLOps / Production Track

Deploying, operating, and maintaining LLM systems in production environments.

Learning Sequence

Follow the numbered steps in order. Each step builds on the previous one to give you a coherent understanding of this topic area.

  1. Chapter 09: Inference Optimization and Efficient Serving (quantization, KV-cache, speculative decoding)
  2. Sections 14.1 through 14.3: Fine-Tuning Basics (when and how to fine-tune for your use case)
  3. Chapter 15: Parameter-Efficient Fine-Tuning (PEFT) (LoRA, QLoRA, adapter merging)
  4. Chapter 26: Agent Safety and Production Infrastructure (guardrails, sandboxing, agent monitoring)
  5. Chapter 29: Evaluation and Experiment Design (LLM-as-judge, A/B testing, benchmarks)
  6. Chapter 30: Observability and Monitoring (drift monitoring, trace analysis, dashboards)
  7. Chapter 31: Production Engineering and Operations (CI/CD for LLMs, guardrails, cost management)
  8. Chapter 32: Safety, Ethics and Regulation (red-teaming, compliance, responsible deployment)
