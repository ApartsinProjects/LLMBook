Course Syllabi: University Programs

How to Use This Section

This section provides four complete 14-week university syllabi. Instructors can adopt a syllabus directly or mix elements from multiple courses. All syllabi assume a 3-hour weekly session (lecture plus lab). Self-directed learners looking for goal-based paths should see FM.2: Reading Pathways instead.

University Course Syllabi

With 36 chapters, no single semester can cover everything. The following four syllabi are designed for instructors adopting this book for a single-semester (14-week) university course. Click any card for the complete week-by-week syllabus with hyperlinked chapter references.

Undergraduate Engineering

Audience B.Sc. CS / Software Engineering
Duration 14 weeks, 3 hrs/wk
Focus NLP foundations, transformers, APIs, RAG, agents

Undergraduate Research

Audience B.Sc. students, strong math background
Duration 14 weeks, 3 hrs/wk
Focus Transformer internals, scaling laws, interpretability, alignment

Graduate Engineering

Audience M.Sc. / M.Eng. production AI
Duration 14 weeks, 3 hrs/wk
Focus Fine-tuning, RAG, multi-agent, eval, observability, deployment

Graduate Research

Audience M.Sc. / Ph.D. LLM theory and frontiers
Duration 14 weeks, 3 hrs/wk (lecture + seminar)
Focus Training dynamics, RLHF/DPO, interpretability, scaling, safety
