Appendix V

LLM Tooling Ecosystem: Comparison and Decision Guide

Big Picture

The LLM tooling ecosystem has grown rapidly, with dozens of frameworks competing across orchestration, agents, evaluation, serving, and fine-tuning. This appendix provides structured comparisons, decision tables, and selection criteria so you can choose the right tool for each layer of your AI stack without trial-and-error across every option.
