Building Conversational AI using LLM and Agents

Part IV: Training and Adapting

Generating data, fine-tuning models, distilling knowledge, aligning with human preferences, and interpreting what models learn.

Part Overview

Part IV is the heart of the book for practitioners who want to customize LLMs. You will learn to generate synthetic training data, fine-tune models (full and parameter-efficient), distill large models into smaller ones, merge model weights, align models with human preferences via RLHF and DPO, and peer inside models using interpretability tools. This is the most technically dense part of the book; take your time with the labs.

Chapters: 6 (Chapters 12 through 17). Builds on API and prompting skills from Part III and supplies the trained models used in Part V and beyond.
