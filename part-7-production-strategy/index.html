Part VII: Production and Strategy

Deploying LLM systems safely at scale, navigating ethical considerations, and building organizational AI strategy.

Part Overview

Part VII addresses everything that happens after the model works in a notebook. You will learn to deploy LLM systems safely and reliably, implement guardrails, handle security threats (prompt injection, data exfiltration), navigate ethical and regulatory considerations, and build organizational strategy around AI products. This part is essential reading for anyone shipping LLM applications to real users.

Chapters: 2 (Modules 26 and 27). The culmination of the book, drawing on technical knowledge from all preceding parts and framing it in a business and ethical context.
